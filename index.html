Six Word Stories

Six Word Stories is a project inspired by a friend. The premise of the project is to express a story in six words only. It may just look like a bunch of nonsense to you but to me this project means a lot. I like this project because it serves an outlet for me to express my inner thoughts and feeling out to the public without people really understanding what I am saying because they have no context. These six word stories are purposely left ambiguous so that you never really understand what I am thinking but it gives me the catharsis that I needed at the time. This project leverages Twitter as the primary medium in which I release my stories.
